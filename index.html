With over five years designing team wear, outerwear, lifestyle and technical apparel I have had the privilege to work with top-tier brands like Scuderia Ferrari, BMW Motorsport, Red Bull Racing, Porsche Motorsport, Team Penske, Alfa Romeo, Kick F1, W Series and Formula 1.

My passion for team sports drives my commitment to excellence in every design project I undertake including concept designs, creative directions and design camps. My work isn't just about designing apparel, it's about advocating for equality and pushing for sustainability in the sports industry. I'm dedicated to supporting women in sports and championing equal opportunities for all sports enthusiasts.

With a keen eye for detail, technical and a love for the latest technology, I'm always looking to innovate and elevate the standards of design for teams and athletes, focusing on both technical performance and sustainability.

Beyond my skills with Adobe Suite and my enthusiasm for 3D design—which I see as crucial for the future of our industry—I'm known for being a reliable and approachable colleague who thrives in a team environment.
