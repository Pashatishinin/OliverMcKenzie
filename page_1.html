Scuderia Ferrari 2024 Miami Special was a story that had been long in the making since 2020.

Brought to life in 2023 while visiting Ferrari trackside.

The concept wrote itself, the origins of Enzo Ferrari favoured colour Azzuro La plata before the infamous Rosso Corsa, early polos and race-suits worn by the like of Surtees, Fangio and Lauda as well as original colour for Ferrari mechanics overall.

Accompanied by stories from the 60s, 70s and being able to bring a fresh vintage outlook to the collection with the shirt silhouette, varsity jacket and rectangle Scudetto, the collection is very impactful.
